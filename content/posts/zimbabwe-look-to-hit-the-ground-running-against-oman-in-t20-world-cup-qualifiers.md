The team, captained by Zeeshan Maqsood, has been working on enhancing their skills and building a competitive edge. With a blend of aggressive batting and strategic bowling, Oman aims to capitalize on their home advantage and make a statement against Zimbabwe.

As both teams gear up for this encounter, the stakes are high. A victory for Zimbabwe would not only boost their confidence but also set the tone for the rest of the qualifiers. Fans and analysts alike will be watching closely as these two teams clash in what could be a pivotal moment in their respective T20 World Cup journeys.
