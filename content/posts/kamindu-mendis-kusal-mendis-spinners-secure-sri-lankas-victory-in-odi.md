Kusal Mendis also contributed significantly, providing vital support in the middle order and ensuring that the innings remained on track. Their partnership proved crucial, as it not only stabilized the innings but also allowed the team to set a challenging target for the opposition.

Their ability to turn the ball and maintain pressure resulted in regular breakthroughs, ultimately leading to a comprehensive win for Sri Lanka.
