# Lauren Down Calls Time on Her Cricket Career

In a heartfelt announcement that has resonated throughout the cricketing community, New Zealand cricketer Lauren Down has officially called time on her playing career. The talented batter, known for her contributions to the White Ferns, expressed mixed emotions about her decision but is looking forward to a new chapter in her life.

## A Fond Farewell to a Stellar Career

Lauren Down, who made her international debut in 2016, has been a vital part of the New Zealand women's cricket team. Over the years, she has showcased her skills in both One Day Internationals (ODIs) and T20 Internationals, leaving an indelible mark on the sport. In her retirement announcement, Down stated, "I will miss being around the girls, but I'm now looking forward to watching on from the sidelines." This sentiment highlights the camaraderie and bonds she formed with her teammates throughout her career.
