Nabi's performance was nothing short of extraordinary, as he claimed 6 wickets for 45 runs in the first innings and followed it up with 6 for 68 in the second. His ability to consistently trouble the opposition batsmen with his spin bowling proved crucial in dismantling Madhya Pradesh's batting order. J&K's bowlers complemented Nabi's efforts well, with the team managing to restrict MP to 140 and 188 runs in their two innings, respectively.

Chasing a target of 300 runs, Madhya Pradesh struggled to build partnerships, with only a few batsmen managing to make notable contributions. The J&K bowlers maintained relentless pressure, and despite a valiant effort from their middle order, the team ultimately fell short. This victory not only secures J&K's place in the semi-finals but also highlights the growing strength and potential of cricket in the region.
