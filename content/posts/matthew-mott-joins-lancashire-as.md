# Matthew Mott Joins Lancashire as T20 Consultant

In a significant development for Lancashire Cricket, former England white-ball coach Matthew Mott has been appointed as a T20 consultant. This strategic move comes ahead of the Manchester Super Giants' upcoming season, where Mott’s expertise is expected to play a pivotal role in shaping the team's performance. 

## A New Era for Lancashire Cricket

Matthew Mott, who previously guided the England men's team to a historic T20 World Cup victory in 2022, brings a wealth of experience and knowledge to Lancashire. His appointment is a clear indication of the club's commitment to enhancing their T20 format performance. With the Manchester Super Giants set to compete in the next edition of The Hundred, Mott's insights will be invaluable in crafting a competitive squad.

Mott's track record speaks for itself. Under his leadership, England transformed into a formidable white-ball unit, known for their aggressive batting and strategic acumen. His ability to nurture talent and foster team cohesion will be crucial as Lancashire looks to make a mark in the T20 landscape.

## Mott's Vision for the Manchester Super Giants

In a statement following his appointment, Mott expressed his enthusiasm for the role. "I am thrilled to be joining Lancashire and working with the Manchester Super Giants. The T20 format is incredibly exciting, and I look forward to bringing my experience to the team," he said. 

Mott's vision includes not only focusing on immediate performance but also developing a long-term strategy that will benefit the club in the years to come. His experience with various T20 leagues around the world, including the Big Bash League (BBL) and the Indian Premier League (IPL), will provide a fresh perspective that can elevate the Super Giants' game.

Lancashire has a rich history in English cricket, and their ambitions in the T20 format are clear. The club has been working diligently to build a squad that can compete at the highest level.
