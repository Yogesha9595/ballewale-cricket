Double Century

Gharami's Maiden Double-Ton Strengthens Bengal's Lead in Domestic Cricket

In a remarkable display of batting prowess, Koushik Gharami scored his maiden double-century, guiding Bengal to a commanding position in their ongoing domestic cricket match against Assam. Gharami’s impressive innings of 201 runs not out has not only bolstered Bengal’s total but also showcased his potential as a rising star in the domestic circuit. Bengal resumed their innings on the second day of the match at Eden Gardens, with Gharami at the crease. His innings was marked by a blend of aggression and patience, as he struck 25 boundaries and 2 sixes, demonstrating his ability to rotate the strike while also punishing loose deliveries. Gharami’s partnership with the middle order was pivotal, as he built crucial stands that allowed Bengal to extend their lead significantly.
