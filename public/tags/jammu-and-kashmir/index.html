Nabi's 12-Wicket Haul Leads J&K to Historic Ranji Semi-Final

In a historic achievement for Jammu and Kashmir, Mohammad Nabi’s extraordinary bowling performance led the team to their first-ever semi-final in the Ranji Trophy, defeating Madhya Pradesh by a significant margin. Nabi’s remarkable 12-wicket haul across both innings was instrumental in securing the victory, showcasing his prowess and experience on the domestic stage. The match, held at the iconic Rajiv Gandhi International Cricket Stadium, saw Jammu and Kashmir dominate from the outset. After winning the toss, the team opted to bowl first, a decision that proved to be pivotal. Nabi, with his left-arm orthodox spin, dismantled the Madhya Pradesh batting lineup, claiming 7 wickets for just 45 runs in the first innings.
