Nabi's 12-Wicket Haul Secures J&K's First Ranji Trophy Semi-Final Spot

In a historic achievement for Jammu & Kashmir cricket, all-rounder Mohammad Nabi delivered a stunning performance, taking 12 wickets in the match to propel his team to their first-ever semi-final in the Ranji Trophy. The quarter-final clash against Madhya Pradesh concluded with J&K emerging victorious by a margin of 85 runs, marking a significant milestone in the state’s cricketing history. The match, held at the iconic Eden Gardens in Kolkata, witnessed a remarkable display of skill and determination from Nabi, who claimed 6 wickets in the first innings and followed it up with another 6 in the second. His exceptional bowling not only dismantled the Madhya Pradesh batting lineup but also showcased his prowess as one of the leading bowlers in the domestic circuit.
