England Bat First as Nepal Introduces Sher Malla in International Debut

In a highly anticipated international fixture, England won the toss and elected to bat first against Nepal, marking a significant occasion for the visiting team as they handed a debut cap to Sher Malla. The match, held at the Tribhuvan University International Cricket Ground in Kathmandu, is part of a bilateral series aimed at enhancing competitive cricket between the two nations. England’s decision to bat first reflects their strategy of setting a challenging target for the home side, with a strong batting lineup that includes seasoned players. The pitch conditions are expected to favor the batsmen early on, providing an opportunity for England to capitalize on the fresh surface.
