Hetmyer Heroics and Shepherd's Hat-Trick Lead West Indies to Victory Over Scotland

In a thrilling opening match of the international series, the West Indies showcased their prowess with a commanding victory over Scotland, driven by Shimron Hetmyer’s explosive batting and Romario Shepherd’s remarkable hat-trick. The match, held at the picturesque venue of Edinburgh, saw the West Indies dominate from the outset, setting the tone for what promises to be an exciting series. Shimron Hetmyer was the standout performer for the West Indies, scoring a blistering 85 runs off just 53 balls. His innings was laced with six boundaries and four towering sixes, demonstrating his ability to accelerate the scoring at crucial moments. Hetmyer’s aggressive approach helped the West Indies post a formidable total of 245 runs for the loss of 7 wickets in their allotted 50 overs.
