Washington Sundar to Join Indian Squad Ahead of Namibia Clash

Washington Sundar is set to join the Indian cricket team in Delhi ahead of their upcoming match against Namibia.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 7, 2026 • 2 min read

In a significant development for the Indian cricket team, all-rounder Washington Sundar is scheduled to link up with the squad in Delhi before their upcoming World Cup clash against Namibia. Sundar, who has been a key player for India in various formats, is expected to bolster the team’s lineup as they aim to secure a crucial victory in the tournament.

Sundar’s inclusion comes at a pivotal time for the Indian side, which is looking to maintain momentum in the World Cup. The all-rounder has been in impressive form, showcasing his skills with both bat and ball in recent domestic and international fixtures. His ability to contribute in multiple facets of the game makes him an asset, especially in the high-stakes environment of the World Cup.

The Indian team management has been closely monitoring Sundar’s progress, and his arrival in Delhi is anticipated to provide a significant boost to the squad. As the team gears up for the match against Namibia, Sundar’s experience and versatility will be instrumental in strategizing against their opponents. With the World Cup reaching its critical stages, every player’s contribution will be vital in determining India’s success in the tournament.

As the match date approaches, fans and analysts alike will be keen to see how Sundar integrates into the team dynamics. His performance could play a crucial role in India’s campaign as they look to navigate through the group stages and advance towards the knockout rounds. The clash against Namibia promises to be an exciting encounter, and Sundar’s presence is sure to enhance the team’s prospects in the ongoing World Cup.
