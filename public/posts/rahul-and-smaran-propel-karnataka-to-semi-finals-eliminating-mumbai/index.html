Rahul and Smaran Propel Karnataka to Semi-Finals, Eliminating Mumbai

Karnataka's stellar performance led by Rahul and Smaran knocks Mumbai out of the tournament, securing a place in the semi-finals.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 9, 2026 • 2 min read

In a thrilling encounter that showcased the best of domestic cricket, Karnataka triumphed over Mumbai, securing their place in the semi-finals of the ongoing tournament. The match, held at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium in Bengaluru, saw Karnataka’s top-order batsmen, particularly captain KL Rahul and young talent Smaran, deliver exceptional performances that ultimately led to their victory.

Karnataka won the toss and elected to bat first, setting a challenging target of 280 runs. KL Rahul, leading from the front, played a captain’s knock, scoring a well-crafted 92 runs off 105 balls. His innings was marked by elegant stroke play and a keen sense of timing, which kept the scoreboard ticking. Smaran, who has been in fine form throughout the tournament, complemented Rahul’s innings with a crucial 75 runs, providing the stability needed in the middle overs. The duo’s partnership was pivotal, allowing Karnataka to post a competitive total despite a late-order collapse.

In response, Mumbai’s chase got off to a shaky start, losing early wickets. The Karnataka bowlers, led by the impressive performance of their pace attack, kept the pressure on the Mumbai batsmen. The spin duo of K. Gowtham and Shreyas Gopal also played a significant role, taking key wickets at crucial junctures. Mumbai’s innings faltered as they struggled to build partnerships, ultimately being bowled out for just 220 runs, 60 runs short of the target. This defeat not only knocked Mumbai out of the tournament but also highlighted Karnataka’s strength as they move forward in the competition.

With this victory, Karnataka has now advanced to the semi-finals, where they will look to continue their strong form. The team will be buoyed by the performances of Rahul and Smaran, who have both shown their capability to perform under pressure. As the tournament progresses, Karnataka will aim to leverage their recent success and strive for a place in the final. The match served as a reminder of the unpredictability of cricket, where a single performance can change the course of a tournament.
