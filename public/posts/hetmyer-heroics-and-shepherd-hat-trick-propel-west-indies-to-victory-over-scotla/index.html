Hetmyer Heroics and Shepherd Hat-Trick Propel West Indies to Victory Over Scotland

Shimron Hetmyer shines with the bat while Romario Shepherd takes a hat-trick as West Indies secure a dominant win against Scotland.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 7, 2026 • 2 min read

In a thrilling display of cricketing prowess, the West Indies secured a commanding victory over Scotland on the opening day of their international series. The match, held at the picturesque venue of Edinburgh, saw Shimron Hetmyer steal the spotlight with an explosive batting performance, while Romario Shepherd’s remarkable hat-trick added to the team’s dominance. The West Indies, known for their flair and aggressive style, showcased their potential ahead of the upcoming international fixtures.

Shimron Hetmyer, returning to form, played a pivotal role in setting up the West Indies’ innings. His aggressive stroke play and ability to find gaps in the field were on full display as he scored a blistering 87 runs off just 65 balls. Hetmyer’s innings was characterized by powerful boundaries and clever singles, allowing the West Indies to post a formidable total of 275 runs. His partnership with the middle-order batsmen was crucial, as they built momentum and kept the scoreboard ticking, putting pressure on the Scottish bowlers.

On the bowling front, Romario Shepherd’s performance was nothing short of spectacular. The all-rounder claimed a hat-trick in the latter stages of the Scottish innings, dismantling their batting lineup with precision and skill. Shepherd’s ability to consistently hit the right lengths and his variations in pace proved too much for the Scottish batsmen. He finished with impressive figures of 4 wickets for just 32 runs, leading the West Indies to bowl out Scotland for a mere 145 runs, securing a victory by 130 runs.

This match serves as a significant confidence booster for the West Indies as they prepare for more challenging encounters in the international arena. The combination of Hetmyer’s batting finesse and Shepherd’s bowling brilliance illustrates the depth of talent within the West Indies squad. As the series progresses, fans will be keen to see if the team can maintain this momentum and build on their success against Scotland.
