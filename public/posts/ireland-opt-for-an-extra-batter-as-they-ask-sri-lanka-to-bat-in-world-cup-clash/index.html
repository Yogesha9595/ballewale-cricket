This choice reflects Ireland’s intent to maximize their scoring potential in the ongoing tournament, as they seek to secure vital points in the group stage. The match, held at the iconic venue, is expected to be a test of both teams’ skills and strategies.

Ireland’s captain announced the decision during the toss, emphasizing the importance of adapting to the conditions and taking advantage of the batting-friendly pitch. Sri Lanka, on the other hand, will look to capitalize on their batting first.
