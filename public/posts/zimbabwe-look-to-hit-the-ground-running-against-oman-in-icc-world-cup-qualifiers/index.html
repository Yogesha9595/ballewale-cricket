Zimbabwe Look to Hit the Ground Running Against Oman in ICC World Cup Qualifiers

Zimbabwe aims for a strong start against Oman in the ICC World Cup Qualifiers, seeking to secure their place in the upcoming World Cup.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 8, 2026 • 2 min read

As the ICC World Cup Qualifiers commence, Zimbabwe is set to face Oman in a crucial match that could significantly impact their journey towards the 2023 ICC Cricket World Cup. The match, scheduled to take place at the iconic Harare Sports Club, is expected to draw considerable attention from fans and analysts alike, as both teams look to establish their credentials in the tournament.

Zimbabwe, having a rich cricketing history, enters this tournament with a blend of experienced players and promising young talent. The team, captained by Craig Ervine, has been preparing rigorously in the lead-up to the qualifiers, focusing on both their batting and bowling units. With key players like Sikandar Raza and Blessing Muzarabani in form, Zimbabwe will be looking to leverage their home advantage to secure a win against Oman. The team’s recent performances in various formats have shown glimpses of their potential, and this match presents an opportunity to translate that into a strong showing on the international stage.

On the other hand, Oman arrives in Zimbabwe with a determination to make their mark in the qualifiers. The team has been steadily improving over the years and has shown that they can compete against higher-ranked teams. Led by their captain, Zeeshan Maqsood, Oman boasts a balanced squad with a mix of experienced players and emerging talent. They will be keen to build on their recent successes and aim for an upset against the hosts. The match promises to be an exciting contest, with both teams eager to start their campaigns on a high note.

As the match approaches, the focus will be on how each team adapts to the conditions and executes their game plans. For Zimbabwe, a strong performance is essential not only for their confidence but also to set the tone for the rest of the tournament. For Oman, this match represents a chance to showcase their growth and ambition in the cricketing world. As the teams prepare to take the field, fans can expect an engaging contest that could potentially shape the trajectory of both nations in the World Cup Qualifiers.
