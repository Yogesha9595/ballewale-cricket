Rahul and Smaran Propel Karnataka to Semi-Final by Eliminating Mumbai

Karnataka's Rahul and Smaran shine as they knock Mumbai out of the tournament, securing a spot in the semi-finals.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 9, 2026 • 2 min read

In a thrilling encounter at the M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Karnataka triumphed over Mumbai by a convincing margin, securing their place in the semi-finals of the ongoing domestic tournament. The match, which saw an impressive performance from Karnataka’s top order, was marked by standout contributions from captain Lokesh Rahul and young sensation Smaran. Their efforts not only led to a comprehensive victory but also spelled the end of the road for the traditionally strong Mumbai side.

Karnataka set the tone early in the match, opting to bat first after winning the toss. Lokesh Rahul, known for his elegant stroke play, anchored the innings with a masterful century, scoring 112 runs off 120 balls. His innings was laced with boundaries and showcased his ability to rotate the strike effectively. Rahul’s partnership with Smaran, who scored a brisk 78 off just 65 deliveries, was crucial in building a solid foundation for Karnataka. The duo’s aggressive approach kept the Mumbai bowlers under pressure, allowing Karnataka to post a formidable total of 290 runs.

In response, Mumbai struggled to find their rhythm against a disciplined Karnataka bowling attack. Despite a valiant effort from their opener, who managed to score 65 runs, the team faltered under pressure. Karnataka’s bowlers, led by the experienced Vinay Kumar, executed their plans meticulously, restricting Mumbai to 220 runs. The victory was sealed with a commanding performance in the field, where Karnataka’s sharp catching and strategic bowling changes played a vital role.

With this win, Karnataka not only advances to the semi-finals but also sends a strong message to the other teams in the tournament. The combination of experienced players like Rahul and emerging talents such as Smaran has positioned Karnataka as a formidable contender. As they prepare for the semi-final clash, the team will look to build on this momentum and aim for the championship title.
