Hetmyer Heroics, Shepherd Hat-Trick Highlight West Indies' Opening Day Win Over Scotland

West Indies secured a convincing victory over Scotland, thanks to Shimron Hetmyer's explosive batting and Romario Shepherd's hat-trick.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 7, 2026 • 2 min read

In a thrilling encounter at the picturesque Edgbaston, the West Indies showcased their cricketing prowess with a commanding victory over Scotland in their opening match of the ICC Cricket World Cup Qualifiers. The match, held on a sun-drenched day, saw Shimron Hetmyer steal the spotlight with a blistering century while Romario Shepherd’s exceptional bowling performance, which included a hat-trick, further solidified the Caribbean side’s dominance.

Batting first, the West Indies posted a formidable total of 320 runs, largely due to Hetmyer’s explosive innings of 135 runs off just 98 balls. Hetmyer, known for his aggressive style, struck 12 fours and 6 sixes, displaying both power and finesse as he anchored the innings. His partnership with Shai Hope, who contributed a steady 65, was instrumental in setting a challenging target for the Scots. The West Indies’ innings was characterized by aggressive stroke play and calculated risks, leaving the Scottish bowlers struggling to contain the run flow.

In response, Scotland faced an uphill battle from the outset. Romario Shepherd’s exceptional bowling performance turned the tide in favor of the West Indies. Shepherd, who had been a key player in the domestic circuit, delivered a stunning spell that culminated in a hat-trick, claiming three wickets in consecutive deliveries. His figures of 5 for 45 dismantled the Scottish batting order, leaving them reeling at 150 all out. The West Indies’ fielding was equally commendable, with sharp catches and tight ground fielding contributing to their comprehensive win.

The victory not only boosts West Indies’ confidence as they aim for a successful campaign in the qualifiers but also highlights the depth of talent within their squad. With Hetmyer and Shepherd leading the charge, the team will look to build on this momentum in their upcoming matches. Scotland, on the other hand, will need to regroup and reassess their strategies as they prepare for their next challenge in the tournament. Overall, this match served as a reminder of the unpredictable nature of cricket, where individual brilliance can often turn the tide in favor of a team.
