Manchester United Owner Joins Bidding War for RCB and RR

Manchester United owner enters the fray to acquire RCB and RR, signaling potential changes in IPL ownership dynamics.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 9, 2026 • 2 min read

In a significant development within the Indian Premier League (IPL), the owner of Manchester United, Avram Glazer, has emerged as one of the bidders for the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) and Rajasthan Royals (RR). This move comes as part of a broader trend of high-profile investments in cricket, particularly in the lucrative IPL franchise model, which has attracted attention from international sports figures and investors alike.

The IPL, known for its vibrant atmosphere and substantial financial rewards, has seen franchises evolve into valuable assets. The potential acquisition of RCB and RR by Glazer could reshape the landscape of the league, which has already witnessed an influx of foreign ownership in recent years. The Glazer family’s involvement in sports management, particularly with Manchester United, adds a layer of intrigue to this bidding process, as they bring a wealth of experience in handling high-stakes sports franchises.

Glazer’s interest in RCB and RR is not just about financial investment; it reflects a growing trend where sports owners seek to diversify their portfolios by investing in cricket, a sport that has gained immense popularity worldwide. The IPL’s unique format and global fanbase make it an attractive proposition for investors looking to tap into the cricketing market. As the bidding process unfolds, it remains to be seen how this will impact the teams’ management and future strategies.

As the IPL continues to expand and evolve, the entry of high-profile investors like Avram Glazer signals a new era for the league. The outcome of this bidding process could have far-reaching implications for the franchises involved, as well as for the overall dynamics of the IPL. With the potential for increased investment and strategic changes, fans and stakeholders alike will be watching closely as this story develops.
