Kamindu Mendis, Kusal Mendis, and Spinners Lead Sri Lanka to Victory

By BalleWale Editorial Team • Feb 8, 2026 • 2 min read

In a thrilling ODI encounter, Sri Lanka secured a convincing victory over their opponents, thanks to the stellar performances from Kamindu Mendis and Kusal Mendis, alongside a formidable display from the spin department. The match, held at the iconic R. Premadasa Stadium in Colombo, saw Sri Lanka chase down a competitive target, showcasing their depth in both batting and bowling.

Kamindu Mendis was the standout performer with the bat, scoring a brilliant century that anchored Sri Lanka’s innings. His composed approach and ability to rotate the strike were pivotal in building partnerships, particularly during a challenging phase of the chase. Kusal Mendis also made significant contributions, providing crucial support and accelerating the scoring rate when required. The duo’s partnership not only steadied the ship but also set the foundation for a successful run chase, ultimately leading Sri Lanka to victory with several overs to spare.

On the bowling front, Sri Lanka’s spinners were instrumental in restricting the opposition’s scoring. The spin trio, comprising of Wanindu Hasaranga, Maheesh Theekshana, and Kamindu Mendis, exploited the turning conditions effectively, taking key wickets at regular intervals. Their ability to bowl in tandem and maintain pressure was evident as they consistently troubled the batsmen, leading to several dismissals through well-executed deliveries. This performance highlighted Sri Lanka’s strength in spin bowling, a traditional hallmark of their cricketing prowess.

The win not only boosts Sri Lanka’s confidence but also solidifies their position in the ongoing ODI series. With the World Cup approaching, performances like these will be crucial as the team looks to fine-tune their strategies and combinations. The contributions from both Kamindu Mendis and Kusal Mendis, along with the spinners, underscore the potential within the squad, setting an optimistic tone for the challenges ahead. As Sri Lanka continues to build momentum, fans will be eager to see how this blend of youth and experience performs in the upcoming fixtures.
