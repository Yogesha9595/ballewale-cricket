Italy Opt to Bowl First as Scotland Introduces Brad Wheal in Squad

Italy has chosen to bowl first against Scotland, who have included Brad Wheal in their lineup for today's match.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 9, 2026 • 2 min read

In a crucial match of the international cricket calendar, Italy has won the toss and opted to bowl first against Scotland. This decision comes as both teams look to make a significant impact in the ongoing series. The match, taking place at a well-prepared venue, is expected to showcase some thrilling cricket as both sides vie for supremacy on the field.

Scotland has made a notable change to their squad by including fast bowler Brad Wheal, who has been in impressive form in recent domestic competitions. His inclusion is anticipated to bolster the Scottish bowling attack, providing them with an extra edge in the early overs. Wheal’s ability to swing the ball and maintain pressure on the batting side will be crucial, especially in the initial stages of the innings. His performance will be closely monitored as fans and analysts alike are eager to see how he adapts to the international stage.

Italy, on the other hand, will be looking to capitalize on their decision to bowl first, aiming to restrict Scotland to a manageable total. The Italian side has shown considerable improvement in recent matches, and their bowlers will be keen to exploit any early movement in the conditions. With a mix of experienced campaigners and young talent, Italy’s bowling unit is prepared to challenge Scotland’s batting lineup.

As the match progresses, both teams will be focused on executing their strategies effectively. For Scotland, setting a competitive target will be essential, while Italy will aim to chase down any score set by their opponents. The encounter promises to be an exciting contest, reflecting the growing competitiveness of international cricket.
