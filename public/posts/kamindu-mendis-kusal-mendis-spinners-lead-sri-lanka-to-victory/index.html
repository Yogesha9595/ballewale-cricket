Kamindu Mendis, Kusal Mendis, Spinners Lead Sri Lanka to Victory

Kamindu Mendis and Kusal Mendis shine with bat and ball as Sri Lanka secures a convincing win in the latest ODI match.

By BalleWale News Desk ‚Ä¢ February 8, 2026 ‚Ä¢ 2 min read

In a thrilling ODI match held at the R. Premadasa Stadium in Colombo, Sri Lanka showcased a dominant performance, led by the impressive displays from Kamindu Mendis and Kusal Mendis, along with a formidable spin attack. The home side secured a convincing victory against their opponents, reflecting their growing prowess in the limited-overs format.

Kamindu Mendis played a pivotal role with the bat, scoring a well-crafted century that anchored Sri Lanka’s innings. His innings was characterized by a blend of aggressive stroke play and calculated defense, allowing the team to set a competitive target. Kusal Mendis, contributing significantly as well, provided the necessary acceleration in the middle overs, ensuring that Sri Lanka posted a total that would challenge any bowling attack.

As the match progressed, Sri Lanka’s spinners took center stage, effectively dismantling the opposition’s batting order. The spin duo of Wanindu Hasaranga and Maheesh Theekshana proved to be particularly lethal, utilizing the turning conditions to their advantage. Their combined efforts not only restricted runs but also picked up crucial wickets at regular intervals, leaving the visitors struggling to build partnerships.

The victory marks an important milestone for Sri Lanka as they continue to build momentum in the international circuit. With the World Cup on the horizon, performances like these will be crucial for the team’s confidence and strategy moving forward. The blend of young talent like Kamindu Mendis and experienced players such as Kusal Mendis suggests a promising future for Sri Lankan cricket as they aim to reclaim their status on the global stage.
