Kamindu Mendis, Kusal Mendis, Spinners Secure Sri Lanka's Victory in ODI

Sri Lanka clinches a crucial ODI win with stellar performances from Kamindu Mendis, Kusal Mendis, and the spin attack.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 9, 2026 • 2 min read

In a thrilling ODI encounter, Sri Lanka secured a convincing victory over their opponents, thanks to standout performances from Kamindu Mendis and Kusal Mendis, alongside a formidable display from the spin bowling unit. The match, held at the iconic R. Premadasa Stadium in Colombo, showcased the depth and resilience of the Sri Lankan squad as they continue to build momentum in the ODI format.

Kamindu Mendis was the star of the show, scoring a brilliant century that anchored the Sri Lankan innings. His composed batting, characterized by elegant stroke play and calculated aggression, helped the team post a competitive total. Kusal Mendis also contributed significantly, providing vital support in the middle order and ensuring that the innings remained on track. Their partnership proved crucial, as it not only stabilized the innings but also allowed the team to set a challenging target for the opposition.

As the match progressed, Sri Lanka’s spinners took center stage, effectively dismantling the batting lineup of the opposition. The spin trio, led by the experienced Wanindu Hasaranga, complemented by the skills of Maheesh Theekshana and Dhananjaya de Silva, exploited the pitch conditions expertly. Their ability to turn the ball and maintain pressure resulted in regular breakthroughs, ultimately leading to a comprehensive win for Sri Lanka. The spinners’ performance not only highlighted their importance in the team setup but also underscored the strategic depth of Sri Lankan cricket.

This victory is a significant boost for Sri Lanka as they prepare for upcoming international fixtures. The combination of Kamindu Mendis’s batting prowess and the effectiveness of the spin attack showcases the potential of this team to compete at the highest levels. With this win, Sri Lanka aims to build on their success and solidify their position in the ODI rankings, setting the stage for an exciting future in the format.
