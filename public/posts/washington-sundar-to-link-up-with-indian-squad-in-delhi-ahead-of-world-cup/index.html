Washington Sundar to Link Up with Indian Squad in Delhi Ahead of World Cup

By BalleWale Editorial Team • Feb 7, 2026 • 2 min read

In a significant development for the Indian cricket team, all-rounder Washington Sundar is set to join the squad in Delhi as they ramp up preparations for the upcoming ICC Men’s Cricket World Cup 2023. Sundar, who has been a vital part of the Indian setup in various formats, brings a wealth of experience and skill to the team, particularly in the spin department. His inclusion is seen as a strategic move to bolster the team’s depth ahead of the highly anticipated tournament.

Sundar’s recent performances in domestic cricket and the Indian Premier League (IPL) have highlighted his capabilities as both a bowler and a lower-order batsman. With the World Cup scheduled to commence shortly, the Indian selectors have opted to reinforce the squad with players who can adapt to different conditions and contribute in multiple roles. Sundar’s ability to bowl economically and provide crucial runs in the middle order makes him an asset as India aims to secure its third World Cup title.

The Indian team, led by captain Rohit Sharma, has been training rigorously in Delhi, focusing on fine-tuning their strategies and building team cohesion. The management is keen to ensure that all players are in peak physical condition and mentally prepared for the challenges that lie ahead in the tournament. Sundar’s arrival is expected to inject additional energy into the squad as they finalize their preparations.

As the cricketing world turns its attention to the World Cup, Sundar’s integration into the team will be closely monitored by fans and analysts alike. With the tournament set to feature some of the best cricketing nations, India will be looking to leverage every available resource to enhance their chances of success. Sundar’s experience in high-pressure situations could prove invaluable as the team aims to navigate the complexities of the World Cup format.

In conclusion, Washington Sundar’s addition to the Indian squad in Delhi marks a crucial step in the team’s preparations for the World Cup. As they gear up for the challenges ahead, the focus will be on building a cohesive unit capable of performing at the highest level. With the tournament just around the corner, every player’s contribution will be pivotal in India’s quest for glory.
