The decision to chase runs rather than set a target reflects Italy’s confidence in their bowling attack, which has shown promise in recent outings. This match is particularly crucial as both teams aim to solidify their standings in the international arena, with Scotland looking to leverage their experience against a spirited Italian side.

Scotland has made a notable inclusion in their lineup, bringing in fast bowler Brad Wheal for his debut.
