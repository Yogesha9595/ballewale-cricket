Italy Opt to Bowl First as Scotland Include Brad Wheal in Lineup

Italy has won the toss and elected to bowl first against Scotland, who have included Brad Wheal in their playing XI.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 9, 2026 • 2 min read

In a crucial T20 international match, Italy has won the toss and opted to bowl first against Scotland at the picturesque venue of Malahide Cricket Club in Dublin. This decision comes as both teams look to secure valuable points in the ongoing series, with Italy aiming to build on their recent performances while Scotland seeks to assert their dominance in the format.

Scotland has made a notable inclusion in their playing XI with the selection of fast bowler Brad Wheal, who has been in impressive form in domestic competitions. Wheal’s ability to swing the ball and maintain pressure on the opposition will be crucial for Scotland as they look to set a challenging target for Italy. The Scottish side, led by their captain, will be hoping that Wheal’s experience can provide the edge needed in the early overs of the match.

Italy, on the other hand, will be looking to exploit the conditions with their bowling attack, which has shown promise in recent outings. The team’s captain expressed confidence in their bowling lineup, emphasizing the importance of early breakthroughs to put Scotland under pressure. The Italian players are keen to showcase their skills on the international stage and are determined to make a mark in this encounter.

As the match unfolds, both teams will be eager to capitalize on their strengths. Scotland aims to set a solid foundation with their batting lineup, while Italy hopes to restrict them and chase down the target effectively. With the stakes high and both teams looking to make an impact, this match promises to be an exciting contest for cricket fans.
