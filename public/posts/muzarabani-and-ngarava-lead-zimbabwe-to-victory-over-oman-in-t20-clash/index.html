Muzarabani and Ngarava Lead Zimbabwe to Victory Over Oman in T20 Clash

Zimbabwe secured a convincing win against Oman, thanks to impressive performances from Muzarabani and Ngarava in the T20 match.

By BalleWale News Desk • February 9, 2026 • 2 min read

In a thrilling T20 encounter held at the Al Amerat Cricket Ground, Zimbabwe emerged victorious over Oman by a significant margin, thanks to the exceptional performances of fast bowlers Blessing Muzarabani and Richard Ngarava. The match, part of a bilateral series, showcased Zimbabwe’s bowling prowess and their ability to capitalize on early breakthroughs.

Zimbabwe won the toss and opted to bowl first, a decision that proved to be advantageous as Muzarabani and Ngarava wreaked havoc on the Omani batting lineup. Muzarabani, with his height and pace, consistently troubled the batsmen, claiming three crucial wickets for just 24 runs in his four overs. Ngarava complemented his efforts with an impressive spell of his own, taking two wickets while conceding only 18 runs. The duo’s combined efforts restricted Oman to a modest total of 112 runs, setting a challenging target for the hosts.

In response, Zimbabwe’s batting lineup faced initial resistance from the Omani bowlers, but they steadily built partnerships to chase down the target. Opener Tinashe Kamunhukamwe played a pivotal role, scoring a brisk 45 runs off 36 balls, while captain Craig Ervine contributed with a steady 30 runs. The Zimbabwean innings was characterized by calculated aggression and sensible shot selection, allowing them to reach the target with 5 overs to spare.

This victory not only boosts Zimbabwe’s confidence but also highlights the effectiveness of their bowling attack, which has been a focal point in their recent performances. As they prepare for upcoming challenges, the team will look to build on this momentum and continue their upward trajectory in international cricket.
