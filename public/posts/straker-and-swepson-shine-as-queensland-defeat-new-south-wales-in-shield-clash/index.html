Straker and Swepson Shine as Queensland Defeat New South Wales in Shield Clash

By BalleWale Editorial Team ‚Ä¢ Feb 8, 2026 ‚Ä¢ 2 min read

In a gripping encounter at the Sydney Cricket Ground, Queensland emerged victorious over New South Wales, with standout performances from fast bowler Jack Straker and leg-spinner Mitchell Swepson. This match, which concluded on October 15, 2023, showcased the depth of talent in Australian domestic cricket and could serve as a preview for the upcoming Sheffield Shield final.

Straker’s exceptional bowling performance was pivotal for Queensland, as he claimed five wickets for just 45 runs in the second innings, dismantling New South Wales’ batting order. His ability to generate pace and movement off the pitch proved crucial, particularly on a surface that offered assistance to seamers. Straker’s efforts were complemented by Swepson, who took three wickets in the first innings and provided vital breakthroughs when New South Wales appeared to be building momentum. Together, their combined efforts restricted the home side to a modest total, ultimately leading to Queensland’s innings victory.

New South Wales, who started the match as favorites, struggled to find their rhythm against a disciplined Queensland bowling attack. Despite a resilient 75 from captain Kurtis Patterson, the team could not recover from early setbacks. Their first innings total of 210 was insufficient, and Queensland capitalized on the advantage, scoring 350 in their first innings. The pressure mounted on New South Wales in the second innings, where they were bowled out for just 145, handing Queensland a significant victory by an innings and 15 runs.

This match not only highlights the impressive form of Queensland’s bowlers but also raises questions about New South Wales’ ability to bounce back as the Sheffield Shield season progresses. With both teams vying for a place in the final, the stakes are high, and performances like those of Straker and Swepson will be crucial in the coming weeks. As the domestic season unfolds, cricket fans will be eager to see how these teams adapt and prepare for what promises to be an exciting conclusion to the Sheffield Shield.
